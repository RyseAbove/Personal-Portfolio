My name is Noah Jeffrey Layton, I am 16 years old, and I am currently an 11th grade student at Clovis High School and am enrolled in the Web Application Development lab at the Center for Advanced Research and Technology (CART). I am the oldest of three children (11 years age gap) and I have a lot of other families. During elementary - middle school, I was never the most social kid and when Covid-19 hit (school shut down), this definitely didn't help. Once school got back in person, I struggled being social and talking to people. However, throughout the last two years, I have become much more social and have made some great friends along the way. As for hobbies, I love playing video games, I collect POP! Figures, and I have been playing the cello for seven years. I have always loved music and playing/creating music. After high school, I plan to go to Fresno City College to complete my general education, and then going to Fresno State and majoring in Computer Science.
